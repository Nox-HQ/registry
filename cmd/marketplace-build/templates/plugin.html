Verification

Releases are signed with Cosign keyless. Verify the published checksum file:

cosign verify-blob \
  --certificate-identity-regexp "https://github.com/{{.Repository | printf "%s"}}/.github/workflows/release.yml@.*" \
  --certificate-oidc-issuer https://token.actions.githubusercontent.com \
  --signature checksums.txt.sig \
  checksums.txt
